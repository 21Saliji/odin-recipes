Coffee Nudge

Treat yourself to a coffee nudge cocktail topped with cream and chocolate sprinkles. It'll warm you from the inside out on a cold, blustery day!

Ingredients

  • 8 fluid ounces coffee flavored liqueur
  • 8 fluid ounces brandy
  • 4 fluid ounces creme de cacao
  • 8 cups hot brewed coffee
  • 2 cups whipped cream, garnish
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. You will need 8 mugs or glass coffee cups. Pour 1 ounce of coffee liqueur into each mug, then add one ounce of brandy and 1/2 ounce of creme de cacao to each serving..
  2. Fill each mug with hot coffee, garnish with a dollop of whipped cream, and top with chocolate sprinkles; serve
